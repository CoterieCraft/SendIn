If you want to send in your textures for CoterieCraft you can do that here. (I hope) All you have to do:

  • Give your MinecraftForum name or the name you want to have mensioned
  • Add a link where I can download your file (Dropbox prefered, Mediafire and MegaUpload is okay)
  • Tell what mod your textures support, if it is vanilla add something like 'vanilla', 'minecraft' or something like that. If it is connected textures or related please call it 'optifine' or 'MCPatcher'
  • Add optional info with what textures you created. If it is a connected texture just say what one block that is.
